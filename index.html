My research spans the areas of Big Data, data management and database systems. My current work has concentrated on scalable and user-friendly data management and analytics with applications in Web knowledge, cybersecurity, power grid and social newtork. My broader interests include data quality, network science, and information visualization. 

To Prospective Students
  • I am looking for highly motivated and hardworking students who are interested in tackling challenges (theory, algorithms, systems) in different areas, including data science, databases, networking, social science, security. If you are interested in these areas and working with me at WSU, please send me an email with your CV, transcripts, publications (if applicable).
