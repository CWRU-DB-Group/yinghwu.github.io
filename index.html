Research Interests: Data and Knowledge Management, Database Theory and Systems, Data Mining, Data and Information Quality. My current research focuses on knowledge modeling, management and quality in complex networks such as cyber-physical systems, Smart Grid, and Social networks. 

To Prospective Students: I am looking for highly motivated and hardworking students who are interested in tackling data challenges (theory, algorithms, systems) in different areas, including data management, networking, social science, security. If you are interested in these areas and working with me at CWRU, please send me an email with your CV (with list of publications, if any) and transcripts.
